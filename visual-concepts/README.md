## Evolution

| Version | Concept | What worked | What didn't |
|---------|---------|-------------|-------------|
| v1 | Four overlapping petal ellipses, Intent Layer at center | Petal overlaps showed intersections. Clean. | Only showed human side. AI intelligence not represented. |
| v2 | v1 + AI engine boxes below + bridge text | Three zones visible. Engines with dotted borders. | Zones not visually distinct. Petals still felt like "human territory." |
| v3 | Three horizontal zones (human / partnership / AI) with boxes | Clear separation. Each petal shows human + AI contribution. | Lost petal overlaps. Intent Layer placement felt odd. |
| v3.5 | Whiteboard: Intent Layer between partnership and AI, UNKNOWN box, solid vs dashed | Honest framing. Today vs tomorrow distinction. | Led to cross-cutting insight: Intent Layer is not a layer. |
| v5 | Spanning boxes for pairs. Width = relationship distance. | Explicit pairs, no Venn needed. | Width implied importance. F+P looked richer than F+T. |
| v6 | Force-balanced diamond (K4 complete graph). Multi-model consensus via Recurate. | All edges equal. Professional. | T-P and F-I crossed center. Labels crowded. |
| v7 | Combination lattice / Hasse diagram. 4 rows: 4+6+4+1 = 15. Nikhil's second whiteboard. | Everything explicit. Equal treatment. Natural funnel. Pascal's triangle. | Connection lines between rows created visual noise. |
| v7b | v7 + bezier connection lines (singles to pairs to triples to center) | Correctly colored lines traced lineage. | 28 lines total. Cluttered. Crossed over card text. |
| v7c | v7b with 3x2 pairs layout (instead of 2x3) | Better vertical flow. | Lines still messy with longer vertical spans. |
| v7d | Test: split-color pair cards with organic wave boundary. Nikhil's whiteboard sketch (C1/C2 wave). | Color IS the lineage. No lines needed. | Test only, not full poster. |
| v8 | Full poster with split-color backgrounds. Inclined wave (67% top to 33% bottom). White partnership zone. 2x2 triples with descriptions. Rich center card. | Clean, readable, professional. Cards pop against white. Lineage encoded in color, not lines. | Current version. |

## Key Insights That Changed the Diagram

1. **v1 to v2:** "The current AI is not really shown anywhere." (Nikhil)
2. **v2 to v3:** "The 3 layers are not visually coming through." (Nikhil)
3. **v3 to v3.5:** "The petals are NOT in the human zone. They ARE the intersection." (Nikhil)
4. **v3.5 to v4:** "The Intent Layer is a cross-cutting concern. And we don't know if these petals will be relevant with new models." (Nikhil)
5. **v4 to v5:** "How do these 4 partnership boxes overlap?" (Nikhil) Spanning boxes tried. Width bias discovered.
6. **v5 to v6:** Multi-model consensus via Recurate: Claude = network graph, GPT = Venn, Grok = UpSet. Both Claude and Grok rejected 4-circle Venn.
7. **v6 to v6b:** "T-P and F-I is not clear." (Nikhil) Curved edges tried. Labels still crowded.
8. **v6b to v7:** Nikhil's second whiteboard: "4 rows converging to 1." Recognized as combination lattice / Hasse diagram. Pascal's triangle: 4+6+4+1 = 15.
9. **v7b/c lines rejected:** Lines create visual noise at any density. 28 lines across 4 rows is too many. "No matter what shape, I do not see a non-cluttered visual." (Nikhil)
10. **v7d to v8:** Nikhil's whiteboard sketch of organic wave boundary between two colors in a card. "Color IS the lineage." Split-color backgrounds encode parentage without lines. Inclined wave (67% top, 33% bottom). Partnership zone to white for readability.

Each insight came from the human. Each implementation came from the partnership. That IS the charter in action.
