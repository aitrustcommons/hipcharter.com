# V3: Three Sovereign Zones

**Date:** March 16, 2026
**Concept:** Three visually distinct horizontal bands: green (Human Intelligence) → warm/coral (Partnership with four petal boxes) → purple (AI Intelligence with engine boxes). Each petal box shows BOTH human contribution and AI contribution. Intent Layer as a box inside the partnership zone. Dotted connectors between zones.

**What worked:** Three zones immediately clear. Color separation strong. Each petal now showed two-intelligence contribution (human: judgment, design / AI: implementation, speed). Future petals as dotted box.

**What didn't:** Lost petal overlaps (boxes don't overlap). Intent Layer placement inside partnership zone felt odd. Nikhil's whiteboard sketch showed it should sit between partnership and AI, not inside partnership. Led to the cross-cutting insight.

**Key insights from this version:**
1. Nikhil: "Human Intelligence is a totally separate entity. The petals are the INTERSECTION, not the human's territory."
2. Nikhil (whiteboard): Intent Layer sits between partnership and AI, with UNKNOWN box alongside it.
3. Nikhil: "What if the Intent Layer is like a cross-cutting concern? And we don't know if these petals will be relevant with new models."
4. Nikhil: "This whole structure is true as of today. We do not know how future concepts will change this whole world."

**This version + the whiteboard sketch triggered the "today vs tomorrow" evolution doc.**
