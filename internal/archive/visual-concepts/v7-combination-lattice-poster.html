The partnership
Where two intelligences meet. Four domains, six pairs, four triples, one center. 15 positions on the map.
4
Domains of partnership (each shows what human brings + what AI brings)

Foundation

Human: judgment, design, direction, product vision, constraint definition
AI: implementation, speed, iteration, code generation, restructuring

Tooling

Human: needs, workflows, ergonomics, capability gaps to fill
AI: build capability during development. Zero AI dependency at runtime.

Pipeline

Human: data quality standards, curation intent, editorial judgment
AI: enrichment, generation, synthesis. Users interact with output, not AI.

Integration

Human: analysis intent, domain logic, architectural decisions
AI: reasoning alongside proprietary APIs, sensors, and live data feeds.
6
Pairwise intersections (every domain connects to every other)

Foundation + Tooling

Built with AI as partner, ships without AI dependency. The most defensible intersection.

Foundation + Pipeline

Designed the enrichment pipeline by hand. Human architects the data flow, AI generates within it.

Foundation + Integration

Architected the system AND AI works inside it at runtime. Full-stack partnership.

Tooling + Pipeline

Tools that surface AI-curated insights without calling AI at runtime. Enriched data, human interface.

Tooling + Integration

Human curation tools inside AI-integrated systems. Annotator on top of an AI platform.

Pipeline + Integration

Data enrichment feeds AI reasoning at runtime. The richest data architecture intersection.
