# V1: Four Overlapping Petals

**Date:** March 16, 2026
**Concept:** Four overlapping ellipses (Foundation, Tooling, Pipeline, Integration) with Intent Layer at center. "Just Intelligence" as ground. Future petals as dashed arc.

**What worked:** Petal overlaps showed intersections. Clean aesthetic. Overlap labels told stories (e.g., "Built with AI, ships without it").

**What didn't:** Only showed the human's architectural decisions. AI intelligence was completely absent. One-sided view of a two-sided partnership.

**Insight that killed it:** Nikhil: "The current AI is not really shown anywhere. How do we signal LLMs vs Scaled AI vs World Models?"

**Visual:** Rendered inline in Daily Ops V6 chat, March 16 2026. SVG with four colored ellipses (teal/blue/amber/purple), center coral ellipse for Intent Layer, overlap labels in intersection zones, "Just Intelligence" quote at bottom, dashed arc for future petals.
